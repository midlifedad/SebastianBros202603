## Detailed Profile

Emma Miller is a Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) and Partner in MNP's Private Enterprise team in Victoria, BC. She has over 15 years of experience in assurance and taxation for corporate, not-for-profit, and personal clients across sectors like real estate/construction, technology, professionals, and charities. Career: Partner at MNP (Jun 2024-present); Senior Manager at MNP (Jan 2023-Jun 2024); Manager at Hughesman Morris Liversedge CPAs (Jul 2018-Jan 2023); Senior Accountant there (Feb 2011-Jul 2018); Tax Professional at H&amp;R Block (2010). Other roles: Board Director, Vancouver Island Construction Association (VICA, Oct 2024-present); Board Director, Imadene Society/Camp Imadene (2022-present, lifelong volunteer/camper/counselor/secretary); Facilitator, CPA Western School of Business (2018-present). Education: BBA Accounting, Camosun College (2013, Dean’s Honour Roll); CPA (2017); CPA Canada In-Depth Tax Program (2023). Services: Tax compliance/planning/restructuring, assurance reviews, business advisory (succession, sales, retirement). Achievements: Academic honors, professional qualifications, board leadership. Personal interests: Camping, motorcycle riding, hiking, walking, boating, Coastline Church. No major public talks/writings found; active on LinkedIn with community posts [MNP](https://www.mnp.ca/en/personnel/emma-miller), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/emma-miller-065666112), [VICA](https://www.vicabc.ca/about-us/vica-board-of-directors/), [Imadene](https://www.imadene.com/board-members).

## How AI & Exponential Technology Impacts Their World

In tax advisory for medium-sized Canadian businesses, AI/exponential tech automates routine compliance (data extraction, return drafting, anomaly detection), accelerates research on complex tax issues, enables predictive planning/scenario modeling for succession/restructuring, and shifts focus to high-value advisory like optimization and risk management. Challenges addressed: Regulatory complexity, manual data handling, time-intensive reviews. Opportunities: Proactive insights, real-time compliance monitoring, enhanced client value via personalized strategies. Agentic AI could autonomously handle multi-step workflows like research-to-filing [Thomson Reuters](https://tax.thomsonreuters.com/blog/how-to-choose-the-best-ai-tax-research-tool/), [KPMG](https://kpmg.com/ch/en/insights/taxes/how-ai-is-transforming-the-tax-function.html).

### AI Tools Recommended

CoCounsel Tax (Thomson Reuters): AI for tax research Q&amp;A, return reviews, drafting letters/memos, identifying savings like cost-segregation—perfect for quick, cited answers in planning/restructuring. Bloomberg Tax AI Assistant: Jurisdiction-specific queries, cross-jurisdictional comparisons for Canadian/US clients in real estate/tech. Blue J/TaxGPT: Specialized tax research. TaxDome: AI reporting/insights for client profitability, firm performance [Thomson Reuters](https://tax.thomsonreuters.com/blog/how-to-choose-the-best-ai-tax-research-tool/), [TaxDome](https://taxdome.com/en-ca/ai-powered-reporting), [Bloomberg](https://pro.bloombergtax.com/insights/tax-automation/how-is-ai-used-in-tax-and-accounting/).

### Agentic Frameworks & Apps

Agentic AI platforms like Thomson Reuters CoCounsel agents or EY AI tax agents: Autonomous execution of complex tasks (e.g., research tax positions, pull data, draft docs, interact via Teams for audits). Workflow tools: TaxDome AI for automated reporting/pipelines; Black Ore Tax Autopilot for end-to-end return prep. Integrate with Microsoft Copilot for client comms/compliance monitoring—ideal for MNP's private enterprise tax workflows [Thomson Reuters agentic](https://tax.thomsonreuters.com/blog/4-things-tax-and-audit-professionals-need-to-know-about-agentic-ai/), [EY](https://www.ey.com/en_gl/insights/tax/how-agentic-ai-can-reshape-your-tax-function), [TX CPA](https://www.tx.cpa/resources/txcpa-magazine/march-april-2026/2026/03/05/ai-in-accounting-2026-from-practical-automation-to-strategic-advantage).

Daily work disrupted/enhanced: Tax return prep (automated drafting/review from docs, prior-year context); compliance filings (anomaly/risk flagging); research (instant summaries/citations vs. hours manual); client advisory (predictive modeling for succession/sales, variance analysis); assurance reviews (full-population data analysis vs. sampling). Frees time for relationship-building, strategic planning with medium-business clients [KPMG](https://kpmg.com/ch/en/insights/taxes/how-ai-is-transforming-the-tax-function.html), [TX CPA](https://www.tx.cpa/resources/txcpa-magazine/march-april-2026/2026/03/05/ai-in-accounting-2026-from-practical-automation-to-strategic-advantage).
