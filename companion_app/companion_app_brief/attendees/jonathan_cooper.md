## Detailed Profile

Jonathan Cooper is a seasoned real estate executive based in Vancouver, BC, Canada. He holds a BA (Hons) in History from the University of Toronto (1999-2004), a Master's Degree in Real Estate from Georgetown University (2017-2019), Executive Education from Sauder School of Business (2010-2015), LCL/GRP certifications from Institute by LeadingRE (2016), and CNE Designation from Real Estate Negotiation Institute (2016). Career: Sales Associate at Vision Critical (2003-2005); VP Operations at Macdonald Real Estate Group (2006-2016), overseeing operations, HR, IT, planning; COO at Holborn Holdings Ltd (2017-2023), leading development/investment; President at Macdonald Realty (2023-2024), managing brokerage/property mgmt/development; current SVP Operations at Strand (Sep 2024-present), Vancouver-based real estate finance/development/investment firm; also COO at Sebastian Brothers (Properties), a Canadian family office focused on private credit, real estate development, private equity; Advisory Committee at EarnUp (2015-present). Notable achievements: Multiple publications in Inman News, Vancouver Sun (e.g., "How My Agent Found the Perfect Home", "The True Cost of the New Tax"); poetry in literary reviews; speaking at LeadingRE conferences, Inman Connect on Asian investment, cross-border transactions. No explicit personal interests found beyond real estate and poetry writing.[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jonathantscooper)
